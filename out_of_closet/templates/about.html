Who we are

We are a group of human rights activists from Singapore who are commited to reduce social stigma towards the LGBT community.

Our mission

To support the pride movement and to make the world a better place by promoting inclusiveness and LGBT acceptance.

Why

Even in the year 2019, society at large is not very accepting of LGBT individuals. This is a pertinent social problem. We believe that in this age of interconnectiveness and progressiveness, discrimination should not be a social issue. Hence, we created this site with the lofty goal of ending hate and discrimination towards the LGBT community.

We also hope to build a welcoming community of people who are kind and supporting towards each other.
